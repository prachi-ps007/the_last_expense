### 🌌 Expense Tracker — A Layered Financial Experience

“Dare to be different — for it is in your individuality that your true strength lies.”

## ✨ Overview

This is not just a typical expense tracker.

It’s a concept-driven financial experience designed around the idea that money is not just numbers—
it’s movement, decisions, patterns, and growth.

Instead of building only features, this project focuses on feeling and perspective—
turning financial tracking into something more intuitive and meaningful.

## 🧠 Core Idea

In a world where most applications feel similar—
optimized, polished, and often generated—

this project aims to bring back individuality.

Your work should carry a piece of you.
That’s what makes it different.

## 🌍 Concept: Layers of Experience

Each section of the app represents a different layer of financial awareness:

🌌 Space (Home)
Represents the vastness of your financial life — the bigger picture.
☁️ Sky (Transactions)
Daily expenses and income — things that come and go.
🚶‍♀️ Crosswalk (Add Transaction)
Every entry is a decision — a step that shapes your direction.
🌱 Garden (Budget)
What you nurture grows — discipline and financial habits.
🔮 Insights (Prophecy)
Understanding patterns and predicting where your choices lead.
🚀 Features
💸 Track income and expenses
📊 Visual financial summaries

📈 Budget tracking with health indicators
🔮 Insights & spending patterns
